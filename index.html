Group Payment

What it does

Collect contributions from multiple people via link or QR; automatically apply payment when goal is met.

How it works

  1. Create a group payment and set a target.
  2. Share link/QR — contributors pay securely.
  3. Once funded, the recharge is applied automatically.

Why it matters

Great for households, events, or pooled payments — removes friction and manual chasing.

Smart Auto-Top-Up

What it does

Automatically tops up numbers when balance or data falls below your chosen threshold.

How it works

  1. Pick threshold and funding source.
  2. Choose bundle preferences.
  3. Auto top-up triggers when threshold is met.

Why it matters

Prevents loss of connectivity and saves time.

Loyalty Marketplace
